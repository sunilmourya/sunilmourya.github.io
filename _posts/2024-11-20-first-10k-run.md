## The Journey Begins

Three months ago, I could barely run 1 kilometer without stopping. Today, I completed my first 10K run!

### Training Stats

- **Time:** 58:32
- **Pace:** 5:51/km
- **Heart Rate:** Avg 162 bpm
- **Feeling:** Accomplished! 🎉

## What I Learned

### 1. Consistency Beats Intensity

I ran 4 times a week, every week. Even slow runs counted. The key was showing up.

### 2. Listen to Your Body

Week 5, I felt a knee twinge. Took 3 days off. Best decision ever. Injury prevention > pushing through pain.

### 3. Mental Game is Real

Around km 7, my legs wanted to stop. My mind said "just one more kilometer." Then another. Then another.

## The Plan Forward

**Next Goal:** Half Marathon in 6 months

**Weekly Schedule:**
- Monday: Easy run (5K)
- Wednesday: Tempo run (7K)
- Friday: Long run (10K+)
- Saturday: Cross-training (cycling/swimming)

## Why I Run

Running isn't just physical fitness. It's:
- Mental clarity
- Problem-solving time (debugging happens on runs!)
- Building discipline that transfers to coding
- Proving to myself I can do hard things

## Tips for Beginners

1. **Start slow** - Walk/run intervals are perfectly fine
2. **Invest in good shoes** - Worth every penny
3. **Track progress** - I use Strava
4. **Find a route you love** - Makes showing up easier
5. **Rest is training** - Recovery days matter
